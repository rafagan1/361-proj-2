Cable vs Streaming TV

Both services have their pros and cons:

  • Monthly cost: Cable TV is more expensive than streaming services such as DirecTV Now,YouTube, Hulu, or Sling TV.
  • Channels: You generally get more channels with Cable TV.
  • Internet: Streaming TV also require a high speed internet connection, while cable does not.
  • Equipment fees:You have to pay a fee for the cable box while streaming service does not require an equipment fee
  • Flexibility:The streaming services offer greater flexiblity for using the product from your phone, computer, or TV.
  • Contract:Cable usually locks you into a 2-yr contract while the streaming service does not.
